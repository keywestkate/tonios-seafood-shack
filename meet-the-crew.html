How It All Started

THE TONIO'S STORY

Tonio's has become a familiar waterfront gathering place in Summerland Key — a spot where fresh seafood, cold drinks, live music and local personalities all come together. The kind of place people find once and keep coming back to.

At Mile Marker 25, you find Tonio's by following the sound of live music drifting over the water, the smell of fresh-caught fish on the grill, or a recommendation from someone who clearly knows their way around the Keys.

The full story behind the Shack — how it started, who built it, and what keeps it going — is coming soon right here. Stay tuned.
