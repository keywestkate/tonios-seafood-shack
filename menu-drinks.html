Signature Cocktails

Made with house-made syrups, fresh-squeezed citrus and actual effort.
The Keys Mule
Dark rum, fresh squeezed lime, house-made ginger beer, mint. Served in a copper mug. This one has a reputation.
Summerland Rum Punch
Light and dark rum, pineapple, orange and passion fruit juice, grenadine float, orange slice. A true Keys classic.
Coconut Old Fashioned
Coconut-washed bourbon, toasted coconut syrup, Angostura bitters, king ice cube. Slow down. You're on an island.
MM 25 Paloma
Tequila blanco, fresh grapefruit and lime, pink salt rim, splash of sparkling water. Light and honestly dangerous.
The Pink Shrimp
Gin, elderflower liqueur, fresh strawberry, lime and a hibiscus salt rim. Sounds silly. Tastes great. Named for the shrimp.
Tinio's Tiki Punch (for 2)
A full bowl: rum, passion fruit, mango, lime, ginger. Served with two straws and questionable judgment.
