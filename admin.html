What you can edit

🏠

Homepage

Announcement bar, hero text, Today's Special / Bar / Stage panels, and CTAs.

📋

Menus

Add, edit, or remove items from any menu — breakfast, lunch, dinner, drinks, happy hour.

🐟

Daily Specials

Create specials by day of the week with price and photo.

🎣

Fresh Catch

Update what's in and what's out today — shows live on the site instantly.

🍣

Sushi Night

Manage the sushi roll menu and feature your best sellers.

🍝

Pasta Night

Add pasta dishes, prices, and mark items as coming soon.

🎸

Live Music & Events

Schedule shows with performer name, date, time, and photo.

📸

Gallery

Upload photos, write captions, and control the order they appear.

⚙️

Site Settings

Happy hour details, location info, phone number, social links.

Changes go live automatically. After you edit anything in the CMS and click Publish, the website updates within about 60 seconds. You do not need to upload any files or contact anyone — it just works.

Tip: Always click Publish (not just Save) to push your changes live. Drafts are saved but not visible on the website until published.
