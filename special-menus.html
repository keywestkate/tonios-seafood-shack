Tonio's Weekly Specials

TONIO'S
SPECIAL NIGHTS

Some nights deserve their own menu. Monday & Thursday Sushi · Tuesday Crab Night · Wednesday Wings Night. A different reason to come back every week.

Every Monday & Thursday

SUSHI NIGHT

Mondays & Thursdays at the Shack

Twice a week, Tonio's rolls out fresh sushi, Keys-inspired combinations and signature favorites. Whether it's a Monday pick-me-up or Thursday night out — we've got you covered.

Sushi Menu
California Roll   Crab, cucumber and avocado
Shrimp Tempura Roll   Fried shrimp, cucumber and avocado
Kani Salad   Shredded crabmeat, cucumber and roe tossed in a spicy dressing
Salmon Roll   Salmon, cucumber and avocado
Spicy Tuna Roll   Spicy tuna and avocado
San Diego Roll   Crab, cucumber and avocado topped with tuna
Lover's Roll   Steamed shrimp, raw salmon and avocado topped with spicy mayo and eel sauce
Spider Roll   Fried soft-shell crab, cucumber and avocado
Pokey Bowl   Spicy tuna, seaweed salad and kani salad served over sushi rice
Maryland Crab Roll   Crab, asparagus and avocado
Philadelphia Roll   Salmon, avocado and cream cheese
Veggie Roll   Avocado, cucumber, asparagus and cream cheese
Summerland Roll   Fried lobster, cream cheese, avocado, kani salad and red tobiko topped with tempura flakes

Menu selections and availability may change based on the freshest ingredients available.

Available Tuesdays

CRAB NIGHT

Every Tuesday at the Shack

Tuesday is all about the crab. From stone crab to snow crab, crab cakes to crab-stuffed anything — if it's crab, it's on the table Tuesday night at Tonio's.

Crab Night Menu
Stone Crab Claws Market Price Chilled or warm, served with mustard sauce
Snow Crab Legs Market Price Steamed with drawn butter and lemon
Crab Cakes   Jumbo lump blue crab, served with remoulade
Crab-Stuffed Shrimp   Jumbo shrimp stuffed with seasoned crab meat, broiled
Crab-Stuffed Grouper   Fresh grouper fillet stuffed with blue crab and herbs
Crab & Corn Chowder   Creamy Keys-style chowder with sweet corn and blue crab
Soft Shell Crab   Lightly fried, served over greens with remoulade
Crab Mac & Cheese   House mac topped with lump blue crab and Old Bay breadcrumbs

Crab availability is subject to season and daily catch. Stone crab is available mid-October through mid-May.

Available Wednesdays

WINGS NIGHT

Every Wednesday at the Shack

Hump day calls for wings. Crispy, saucy and piled high — Wednesday nights at Tonio's are all about the wings. Pick your sauce, grab a cold beer and settle in.

Wings Night Menu
Classic Buffalo   Hot sauce and butter — the original, done right
Garlic Parmesan   Roasted garlic, real parm, fresh herbs
Honey Old Bay   A Florida Keys original — sweet heat with a coastal kick
Mango Habanero   Tropical sweet and blazing hot — island style
BBQ   Slow-smoked flavor, tangy house BBQ sauce
Naked (Dry Rub)   House-seasoned, crispy fried with no sauce — Tonio's dry rub
Tenders   Hand-breaded chicken tenders, choice of dipping sauce
Loaded Wing Platter   Your choice of two sauces with celery, carrots and house ranch

Wing sauces and specials may vary weekly. Ask your server about Wednesday's featured sauce and deals.
