Happy Hour at Tonio's

Monday through Friday, 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Dine in only at the bar and dock seating. Not available on Florida state or federal holidays. Live music most Wednesdays — check the board when you walk in.

Good to Know

When is Happy Hour?
Monday through Friday, 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Happy Hour is not available on weekends or holidays.
Is Happy Hour available for takeout?
Happy Hour prices apply to dine-in orders only at the bar and dock seating areas.
Is there live music during Happy Hour?
Most Wednesdays feature live music starting at or just after Happy Hour ends. Check our music schedule or call us to confirm tonight's entertainment.
Do you take reservations?
Tonio's is walk-in only. We do not take reservations. Dock and bar seating is first come, first served. Come early on Fridays — it fills up fast.
