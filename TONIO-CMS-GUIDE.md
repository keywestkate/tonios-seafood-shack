# Tonio's Website — Owner's Guide

This guide tells you everything you need to update the website yourself.
No tech knowledge needed. If you can type, you can do this.

---

## How to Log In

1. Open your browser and go to: **https://tonios-studio.vercel.app**
2. Click **"Log in with Google"** (or email if you set up a password).
3. You'll land on the Tonio's dashboard — a list of sections on the left.

That's it. You're in.

---

## How to Add or Edit a Daily Special

Daily specials show up on the homepage under "Today's Special."

1. In the left sidebar, click **Daily Specials**.
2. To add a new one, click the **+ icon** at the top right.
3. Fill in:
   - **Special Name** — e.g. "Grilled Hogfish Plate"
   - **Day of the Week** — pick from the dropdown, or "All Week"
   - **Description** — describe it the way you'd tell a customer
   - **Price** — e.g. `$24` — leave blank if you don't want a price shown
   - **Photo** — drag and drop a photo (optional but looks great)
   - **Show on website** — toggle ON to make it live
4. Click **Publish** in the top right corner.

Done. The special is live on the site within 60 seconds.

**To edit an existing special:** Click on it in the list, make your changes, click Publish.

**To remove a special temporarily:** Click it, toggle **"Show on website"** to OFF, click Publish.
(This hides it without deleting it — you can turn it back on anytime.)

## How to Update Today's Fresh Catch

The fresh catch list on the website shows what's in, what's out, and what's coming.

1. In the sidebar, click **Fresh Catch**.
2. Each seafood item is its own card. Click the one you want to update.
3. Change the **Availability** dropdown:
   - **Available Today** — shows as "in" (green)
   - **Sold Out** — shows as "out" (red)
   - **Coming Soon** — shows a "call ahead" note
4. You can also update the **description** or note.
5. Click **Publish**.

**To add a new fish or seafood item:** Click **+**, fill in the name and status, click Publish.

**Tip:** Update the fresh catch every morning before 11 AM so customers know what to expect for lunch.

## How to Add a Live Music Event

1. Click **Live Music & Events** in the sidebar.
2. Click the **+** to create a new event.
3. Fill in:
   - **Event Title** — e.g. "Live Music Night" or "Trivia Night"
   - **Performer / Band Name** — e.g. "The Sunset Kings"
   - **Date** — use the date picker
   - **Start Time** — e.g. `8:00 PM`
   - **Description** — a line or two about the show
   - **Photo** — upload a performer photo if you have one
   - **Show on website** — toggle ON
4. Click **Publish**.

The event appears on the live music page and (if you toggle "Feature on Homepage") on the homepage too.

**To cancel a show:** Open the event, toggle **"Mark as Cancelled"** to ON, click Publish.
The event will still show but with a "Cancelled" label so people know.

## How to Upload a Photo to the Gallery

1. Click **Gallery** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+** to add a new photo.
3. Click the image area and drag/drop a photo, or click to browse your files.
4. (Optional) Add a **caption** — e.g. "Stone crab season at the dock"
5. Set a **Sort Order** number — lower numbers show first (e.g. 1, 2, 3...)
6. Make sure **"Show in gallery"** is toggled ON.
7. Click **Publish**.

**To remove a photo from the gallery:** Open it and toggle **"Show in gallery"** to OFF, click Publish.
The photo stays saved but disappears from the site.

## How to Update the Announcement Bar

The announcement bar is the scrolling strip at the top of the website.

1. Click **Homepage** in the sidebar (under the house icon).
2. Find the **"Scrolling Announcement Bar Messages"** field.
3. Each line is one announcement. Click a line to edit it.
   - To add a new one, click **"Add item"**
   - To remove one, click the trash icon next to it
   - To reorder, drag the lines up and down
4. Click **Publish** when you're done.

**Examples of what works well:**
- `🐟 Today's Fresh Catch: Hogfish · Yellowtail · Shrimp`
- `🍺 Happy Hour 3–6 PM — $4 drafts · $6 wells`
- `🎸 Live Music Tonight · 8 PM · No Cover`

## Important Tips

### Always click Publish — not just Save

When you make a change, you'll see two buttons: **Save** and **Publish**.

- **Save** saves a draft. It is NOT visible on the website.
- **Publish** makes it live. This is what you want.

Always click **Publish** when you're done editing.

---

### Changes take about 60 seconds to appear

After you publish, the website rebuilds automatically.
Wait about a minute, then refresh your browser to see the change.

---

### You can't break anything

If you make a mistake, you can always go back and fix it.
Every change is saved as a version — nothing is permanently deleted unless you choose to delete it.
