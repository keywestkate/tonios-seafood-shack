PRIVATE EVENTS
AT THE SHACK

Birthdays, rehearsal dinners, fishing trips, corporate outings — Tonio's is built for good gatherings. We have waterfront space, fresh seafood, a full bar and the kind of atmosphere you can't fake.

We keep it simple: tell us your group size, what you're celebrating and when you need it. We'll take care of the rest.

  • Groups of all sizes — call to discuss capacity
  • Custom menus available for private bookings
  • Full bar service included
  • Waterfront dock seating available
  • Live music arrangements on request
